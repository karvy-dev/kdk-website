Accelerators & Enablers

Omni Channel

Customers expect to receive service from your organization on the channel of their choice e.g. phone, email, chat, web, mobile or social media. Companies are providing service on all of these channels – but too often, those channels may exist in silos. This multi-channel service can be taken to the next level with an omni-channel solution that integrates channels to provide a consistent customer experience.
Karvy DigiKonnect has helped businesses by deploying omni-channel solutions making it easy for customers to engage with your business, where and when it’s right for them. Channels are connected so conversations are seamless, agents are more productive, and information can be shared across your company - all without losing focus.

Social Media Solutions

In today’s digital age, when customers are online 24*7, social media serves as "the platform" for customers to voice their opinion, share feedback, post complaint and most importantly works as the primary arena for influencing a customer's decision making process.
Social media solutions are part of Karvy DigiKonnect’s omni-channel customer contact center services, which track customer's opinions to assess their expectations, and support clients in delivering the right-fit service.

Analytics

In our data-filled, customer-centric, digital world, effective decision-making is being driven increasingly by analytics.
Karvy DigiKonnect provides the most comprehensive Analytics Life Cycle solutions. Our multi-dimensional Analytics Solutions give you the power to model your business, predict the outcomes, and prosper in your strategy. From discovery, to planning and execution, we cover your analytics requirements with our wide array of service offerings.

Automation

It is a constant endeavour for companies to improve productivity, reduce errors and minimize costs to remain competitive in today’s tough business environment.
Karvy DigiKonnect possesses the expertise to take your business processes to the next level while you focus on core your competencies. Our extensive automation experience in back office operations helps in reducing TAT, and improving visibility into the status of requests from the front office, the customers, standardization of the process, and the improved quality of services.
